Activities

There are many many activites within this building. There are also dolphins and whale watching from your seats. There is also dolphin meeting and you can swim and touch them.

While you are walking through the aquarium, there might be areas where the staff allows a session where you can touch and hold jelly fishes or sting rays! Do not worry, we only allow poisonous ones and ones that won't sting.

There are also activities for children to attend to learn more about specific marine creatures. Additionally there are playgrounds and toys for children to entertain themselves while the parents have a break.

There are also food and restaurants in case any one gets hungry.


Many of our activities are supervused for the protection and safety of the people around them.

We provide many emergency kits and exit doors in case of emergencies.
