About Us

Self photo taken by the CEO.

Hello, my name is Stacy Chieng. I am the CEO of AquaHome aquarium. I have a PHD in marine biology and a PHD in information technology. I attended George Mason University for over ten year. It's always been a dream of mine to open my own aquarium. I am very excited to share this beautiful place with everyone. I truly hope everyone enjoy this and has an amazing experience.

AquaHome was built in 2018 and it is finally opening on December 1st, 2019.

It took 3.7 million dollars to create this faculity. It wouldn't be possible without the donations and support from the community.
